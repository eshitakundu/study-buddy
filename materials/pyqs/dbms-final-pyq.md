# PYQ

Q1. Explain First Normal Form, Second Normal Form, and Third Normal Form with suitable examples. [8 marks]

Q2. A relation R(A, B, C, D) has the dependencies A → B, B → C, and A → D. Identify a candidate key and state whether the relation is in 3NF. Justify your answer. [6 marks]

Q3. Discuss Boyce-Codd Normal Form. How is BCNF stricter than Third Normal Form? [5 marks]

Q4. What are the ACID properties? Explain why atomicity and durability are both required in an online payment system. [6 marks]

Q5. Describe lost update, dirty read, and non-repeatable read. Use a short example for each. [6 marks]

Q6. Explain Two-Phase Locking. Distinguish between the growing phase and the shrinking phase. [5 marks]

Q7. What is deadlock? Construct a two-transaction example that produces a deadlock. [4 marks]
